# Sample Question Bank

> **IMPORTANT**: These are SAMPLE questions to inspire your thinking. The best questions emerge from the specific context of the work being reviewed. Use these as patterns or starting points, but craft questions that directly address the unique gaps, risks, and opportunities in the actual deliverable. Never ask questions just because they're on a list.

## Universal Clarifying Questions

### Goals & Success

- What does wild success look like for this?
- How will you measure whether this worked?
- What's the single most important outcome?
- If you could only achieve one thing, what would it be?
- What happens if this fails?

### Context & Constraints

- What's driving the urgency on this?
- What can't change?
- What's the budget/resource envelope?
- Who needs to approve this?
- What are the non-negotiables?

### Stakeholders & Impact

- Who's most affected by this?
- Who could block this?
- Who's your champion?
- What's the political landscape?
- How does this affect adjacent teams?

## Domain-Specific Questions

### Product Strategy

- How does this fit the product vision?
- What user problem does this solve?
- What's the evidence of user need?
- How does this compare to competitor solutions?
- What's the adoption strategy?
- How do we validate success early?
- What features are we explicitly NOT building?

### Technical Implementation

- What's the technical debt impact?
- How does this affect system architecture?
- What are the security implications?
- How do we handle migration?
- What's the rollback plan?
- What monitoring do we need?
- How does this scale?

### Go-to-Market

- What's our differentiation?
- Who's the ideal customer?
- What's the pricing strategy?
- How do we create urgency?
- What's the sales enablement plan?
- How do we handle objections?
- What's the competitive response?

### Operations & Process

- Where are the handoffs?
- What's the steady-state operation look like?
- Who owns this long-term?
- What's the documentation plan?
- How do we train people?
- What are the SLAs?
- How do we handle exceptions?

### Team & Organization

- Who needs to be involved?
- What skills do we need?
- How does this affect team dynamics?
- What's the knowledge transfer plan?
- Who's the DRI (directly responsible individual)?
- What dependencies exist on other teams?
- How do we maintain momentum?

## Probing Deeper

### When Something Seems Missing

- "I notice you haven't mentioned [X]. Is that intentional?"
- "Help me understand how [Y] factors in here."
- "What's your thinking on [Z]?"
- "Have you considered [specific risk]?"
- "How does this account for [scenario]?"

### When Assumptions Need Validation

- "What evidence supports that assumption?"
- "How confident are you in that?"
- "What would change if that wasn't true?"
- "Have we validated this with [stakeholder]?"
- "What's plan B if that doesn't work?"

### When Clarity is Needed

- "Can you give me a concrete example?"
- "What does that look like in practice?"
- "Walk me through a typical scenario."
- "How would you explain this to [specific audience]?"
- "What's the simplest version of this?"

## Red Flag Questions

### Strategic Red Flags

- Why hasn't this been done before?
- What's different now that makes this possible?
- Who's tried this and failed? Why?
- What's the opportunity cost?
- Is this solving the right problem?

### Execution Red Flags

- Do we have the right team for this?
- Is the timeline realistic?
- Are dependencies clearly mapped?
- What's most likely to go wrong?
- How do we know if we're off track?

### Market Red Flags

- Will customers actually pay for this?
- Is the market big enough?
- Are we too early or too late?
- What regulatory issues exist?
- Can competitors easily copy this?

## Calibration Questions

### Scope & Ambition

- Is this ambitious enough?
- Are we over-engineering?
- Could we start smaller?
- What's the MVP version?
- Where can we reduce scope without losing core value?

### Risk & Confidence

- What keeps you up at night about this?
- Where are you least confident?
- What would de-risk this?
- What early signals would indicate problems?
- How reversible is this decision?

### Priority & Timing

- Why now vs. next quarter?
- What are we NOT doing because of this?
- How does this rank against other initiatives?
- What happens if we delay?
- What accelerates if we succeed?

## Questions by Deliverable Type

### For Presentations

- Who's in the room?
- What decision are you seeking?
- What objections do you anticipate?
- What's the one thing they must remember?
- How technical should this be?

### For Documentation

- Who's the primary reader?
- When will they read this?
- What decision/action should this enable?
- How often will this be referenced?
- Who maintains this going forward?

### For Proposals

- What's the approval process?
- Who are the decision makers?
- What are their hot buttons?
- What alternatives are being considered?
- What would cause an immediate "no"?

### For Reports

- What decisions will this inform?
- What's surprising or unexpected?
- What action items emerge?
- Who needs to see this?
- How does this compare to expectations?
